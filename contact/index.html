Contact

This ministry welcomes correspondence from those who have engaged with the work published here, from clergy and parish leaders considering an invitation for preaching, teaching, or retreat direction, and from anyone whose inquiry is offered in good faith and with serious purpose. Every letter received is read with attention.

contact@scriptures.us

A few notes on what to expect. Inquiries regarding ministry engagements—preaching, teaching, retreats, or theological counsel—will receive a substantive reply, ordinarily within one to two weeks. Correspondence of a pastoral or theological nature is welcomed and will be read carefully, though the volume of correspondence does not always permit an individual response to every letter. If your inquiry concerns a specific engagement with a date or deadline, please mention this so that it may be given appropriate priority.

Please note that this ministry is not in a position to review unsolicited manuscripts or to provide endorsements. Commercial inquiries are respectfully declined.
