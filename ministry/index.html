Ministry

The work of this ministry extends beyond what is published here. Rev. Basilius Magnus is available for preaching, teaching, retreat direction, and other forms of service to the Church. What follows describes the kinds of engagements this ministry offers.

Preaching

Guest preaching for Sunday worship, feast days, special liturgical occasions, and parish missions. The preaching of this ministry is rooted in the lectionary texts and shaped by sustained engagement with the scriptural, patristic, and theological traditions of the Church.

Teaching

Adult education, parish study series, clergy conferences, and seminary or academic lectures. Topics may include biblical theology, the patristic reading of Scripture, sacramental theology, the theology and practice of preaching, and contemplative approaches to the biblical text.

Retreat Direction

Parish retreats, clergy retreats, and quiet days centered on Scripture, prayer, and theological reflection. These retreats are contemplative in character and designed to draw participants into deeper encounter with the Word of God through sustained reading, silence, and guided meditation.

To inquire about any of these engagements, or to discuss other possibilities for collaboration in ministry, please write through the Contact page. All inquiries are received with care and given a timely response.
