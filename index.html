Scriptures.us

Sacred Scripture, Theological Reflection, and the Ministry of the Word

Sacred Scripture does not yield its depth to haste. This site gathers the work of a ministry given to the slow, patient reading of the biblical text and to the theological labor that follows from taking that text seriously: homilies preached from the lectionary, meditative reflections on Scripture and doctrine, and sustained exegetical studies of specific biblical passages and books. The work is rooted in the patristic and sacramental traditions of the Church. It is offered to readers who bring to their own encounter with the Word the same seriousness with which it was written.
